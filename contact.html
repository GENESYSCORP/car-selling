About Car Soko

Your Trusted Luxury Car Dealer in Kisumu

Since 2008, Car Soko has been the premier destination for luxury vehicles in Kenya. We pride ourselves on offering only the highest quality vehicles with complete transparency and exceptional customer service.

Our showroom in KISUMU features the latest models from top global brands, all carefully inspected and certified by our team of experts.

Visit Our Showroom

Our Journey

2008 - Founded

Car Soko was established with a small showroom in Nairobi, specializing in Japanese imports.

2012 - Expanded to Luxury

We began offering European luxury vehicles, becoming one of the first dealers in Kenya to specialize in premium brands.

2015 - New Showroom

Moved to our current 10,000 sq ft facility in Westlands with state-of-the-art inspection center.

2020 - Online Platform

Launched our digital platform allowing customers to browse inventory and book services online.

2023 - 1000+ Cars Sold

Celebrated our 15th anniversary having sold over 1000 luxury vehicles to satisfied customers.
